LoyaltyChess Rules

  • The Betrayal Rule: If a piece is under attack, it MUST be protected or moved. If left undefended, it becomes a "Traitor".
  • Traitors: The opponent can use the traitor piece for ONE move instead of capturing it.
  • Limitations: A traitor piece cannot deliver a check (Check) or a checkmate (Mate).
  • Sacrifice: A traitor can capture any of its original owner's pieces. After the betrayal move, the traitor piece is removed from the game.
