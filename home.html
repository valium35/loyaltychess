LoyaltyChess Rules

  • The Betrayal Rule: If a piece is threatened and left undefended, it becomes a traitor. The opponent can use it for ONE move.
  • Exceptions: Pawns and Queens are exempt from betrayal. Only Rooks, Knights, and Bishops can betray.
  • Traitors: Cannot deliver a check/mate. After their move, they are removed from the game.
  • Standard Moves: Standard chess rules apply for King, Queen, and Pawns.
