LOYALTYCHESS

The only arena where betrayal is a legal move.

⚡ 1. Active Threat

Betrayal only triggers on fresh threats. If your opponent attacks your piece and you don't defend it, it may turn. Passive or self-sacrificed pieces are immune.

🔄 2. The Choice

Your opponent can use the betrayed piece for one move. Knight, Bishop, and Rooks can betray. Queens and Pawns are always loyal.

💨 3. Final Mission

A traitor can capture allies but cannot Check or Mate. After its move, the piece is removed from the board forever.
